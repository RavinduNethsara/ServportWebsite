Introduction

PROCESS REENGINEERING IN OPTIMIZATION OF THE PROCESS IN VEHICLE SERVICE CENTERE

The business process optimization is the automated enhancement of business processes using pre specified performance measures. The use of digital services (i.e. web app, mobile app) is one of the most recent trends in business process optimization. When it comes to the vehicle service industry, service automation helps in achieving multi-dimensional and helps in streamlining the service process. There are lots of benefits that a business process optimization strategy can bring to the vehicle industry. Reduction of repetitive tasks, increased productivity, greater competitiveness, reduced response time, ensures compliance, performance monitoring, cost reduction, standardization and reduction of failures. People's daily activities in the modern world are heavily influenced by information and communication technology (ICT). Technology's rapid growth over the last several years has altered society on a global scale. People's quality of life and business expansion has benefited from technological advancements. Studies suggest that technology has a good impact and growth in a variety of industries, including education, agriculture, finance, and health. In the same way, the use of ICT in business is tailored to match the organization's demands and builds an integrated basis for efficient business operations.

LITERATURE SURVEY

In the service business, customer happiness is a critical factor in determining a company's success. In order to remain competitive in this market, businesses must work hard to both acquire new consumers and keep their current ones happy. According to several research studies, an increase in firm performance is directly linked to improved customer satisfaction, according to several research [11]. It will eventually bring in much money. As a result, improving customer retention and decreasing customer churn requires a keen eye on consumer behavior [12]. It is more cost-effective to keep current customers than to bring in new ones when it comes to client retention. As a word originated from customer behavior theory, loyalty indicates how customers view services, activities, and brands [13]. Loyalty is a two-fold concept with behavioral and cognitive components. A customer's intention to buy again, switch brands, or donate money to a charity is their behavioral intent. Cognitive is more relevant to customer preferences and recommendations for industry [14]. In order to keep a customer, it is critical that they are happy with their purchase. According to the study, independent variables such as customer service and price justice play a significant role in making customers happy. It is important to note that each independent variable influences the dependent variable in this case. Your business will benefit significantly from delighted customers, who will promote positive wordof-mouth about your brand and help build your brand's reputation. [15] The long-term relationship between the brand and its delighted customers will be mutually beneficial. Customer satisfaction varies between industries and countries for a variety of reasons. For a VietinBank, a study in Vietnam uncovered fifteen elements that influence customer happiness. Website and app performance, personalization, engagement in real-time, and mobility are just a few of these considerations [16]. Customers' pleasure with their online buying experience is influenced by various factors, including the quality of the goods, the brand, and the shopping experience itself [17]. The quality of the cuisine, the quality of the service, and the restaurant's environment all have a role in customer satisfaction, according to a recent Pakistani journal publication [18]. Several elements affect customer satisfaction in the automobile service industry. An investigation was carried out in vehicle service facilities by J. Hong and B. Kim found that customer happiness is influenced by service quality, long-term relationships and relationship advantages, and customer satisfaction and trust [19]. According to C.Randhawa, a model he developed expands on Yee and Faziharudean's thesis that service quality, preserved value, trust, habit, reputation, and servicescape affect customer satisfaction [20][21]. Considering all these points, efforts have been made to develop an effective prediction model to predict whether the customer will retain or churn and its impact on company profitability considering factors affecting customer satisfaction. Because customer retention will vary depending on the identified factors affecting for customer satisfaction. Typically, the service center will suggest the next service date based on the kilometers traveled. However, customers often forget, or it may be changed depending on the type of used engine oil and the vehicle's condition. As technology advances on a daily basis, progressing with technology is more beneficial for any industry. As a solution, machine learning methods were chosen to predict the next service date in the 'Servport' vehicle service management system using a proper dataset. Numerous variables can be used to predict the next scheduled service date. The primary factors include the type of service, the cost of the service, time taken for the service, the average vehicle usage, and the year the vehicle was manufactured. Additionally, the system was developed using a vehicle service history dataset from a reputed vehicle service center in Sri Lanka. After predicting the next service date, the system will notify customers via email of the service date and estimated price. Predicting the next service date benefits not just the consumer, but also the company. It may also be used as a sales technique to encourage customers to return for their next car servicing. Additionally, it results in forming a bond between the customer and the company. Vehicle repair shops are also seriously researching ways to cut down on idle time for their employees. The waste of raw materials must also be reduced. In addition, the raw materials used daily will have an impact. As a result, client satisfaction will be lowered. The magazine produced by the IEOM Society International stresses the importance of anticipating employee performance [22]. Predicting the future is becoming increasingly important in the modern corporate sector because of the rapid changes that affect even the most established structures and the need for precise and realistic measured values into the future. It is a sign of survival and the language of business in the world that forecasts are made. When predicting the future, forecasting is both art and science. The most frequently occurring variable is a person's desire for a product or service. The supply and pricing of a product can also play a role [23]. Predicting future values of variables under study is known as forecasting. Demand and price forecasting have been the subject of several investigations. Product pricing and demand are typically predicted using historical data. In Mauritius, prices were predicted but have since changed. Comparing the prediction results of different technologies demonstrated that the cost of these solutions might be pretty comparable. It was discovered that the critical algorithm and method could not classify and predict numerical values. According to the findings of this study, a small sample size did not yield good prediction precision. [24]. Considerations such as these must be considered before a company selects a forecasting strategy that works best for it. Qualitative, time series, causal, and simulation forecasting approaches are examples of this method [25]. Time series analysis relies on the features of the demand time series to anticipate accurately. Stable and periodic patterns in transition curves resulted in high predicting accuracies; irregular patterns resulted in lower forecasting accuracy. [26].
